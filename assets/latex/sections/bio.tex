\begin{rSection}{Bio}
Dr. Zhaomin Wu is a Research Fellow at the Department of Computer Science, National University of Singapore (NUS). He earned his Ph.D. in Computer Science from NUS in 2024, advised by Prof. Bingsheng He, and obtained a Bachelor's degree from Huazhong University of Science and Technology in 2019. Dr. Wu's research focuses on trustworthy machine learning, with specific interests in trustworthy machine learning, federated learning, and multi-agent system. He received the Singapore NRF Postdoctoral Award in 2026. His work has been recognized with awards such as the SIGMOD Honorable Mention for Best Artifact (2023). His publications appear in top-tier venues including NeurIPS, ICLR, SIGMOD, KDD, WWW, ACL, EMNLP, AAAI, MLSys, and TKDE.
\end{rSection}
